About us

Who are we?

We are a dedicated team of Iranians passionate about sharing our experience in competitive programming. We advocate for personalized private classes with smaller groups, recognizing that everyone has a unique learning style and pace. Our educators are not only experienced but also adept at tailoring their methods to best suit each student. With backgrounds in both competing and coaching at the Iranian and International Olympiads in Informatics (IOI), we have witnessed the profound impact competitive programming can have on an individual.

Let's take a look at Iran's performance in IOI (until 2023):

  1. Iran made its first appearance in IOI in 1992 and has participated for 30 years with 111 contestants representing the country over this period.
  2. Iran has secured 31 gold medals, 65 silver medals, and 23 bronze medals in IOI.
  3. Iran hosted IOI in 2017.

Who is Algoritmi?

Al-Khwarizmi, a 9th-century Persian mathematician, is often hailed as the father of algebra. His groundbreaking work laid the foundation for systematic mathematical problem-solving, leading to the development of the concept we now recognize as an algorithm. Interestingly, the term algorithm is derived from the Latinized version of his name, Algoritmi, which is also the inspiration for us.

Discover more about Al-Khwarizmi in this captivating 3-minute BBC documentary.

Why should I study algorithms?

Algorithms are not just a topic in computer science; they teach us a way of thinking that can be utilized in various fields like Machine Learning (ML), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and software engineering.





Algorithm in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence

Imagine you are writing a program for a machine learning algorithm. If you can't analyze its time complexity, you might end up with code that is correct but takes an impractically long time to execute! Analyzing and optimization are the very first skills you'll learn in this area.

Algorithm in software engineering

Moreover, if you are aiming to join top-tier companies, they definitely ask algorithmic questions during interviews and consider this a necessary skill. By mastering algorithms, your chances of being hired by these companies exponentially increase.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    At what level are the classes held?

    All levels

    We have expert teachers for all levels. It is enough to be familiar with the basic concepts of programming in any language. For example, functions, loops, and simple data structures like arrays.
    While these concepts are teachable, numerous excellent resources are also available online. If you are not familiar with these concepts, you can use our counseling sessions, get a path to the basic concepts and just after that, join our classes.

    I'm not good with English, what should I do?
